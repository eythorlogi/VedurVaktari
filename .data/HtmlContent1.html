Tips to avoid accidents or damage the rental vehicle:

  • Park the vehicle in places where the wind direction is blocked by buildings
  • Take extreme care when opening / closing doors
  • Avoid travel while the worst of the weather is passing
  • If impossible to avoid travel, match driving speed to the conditions
  • Always wear your seatbelt
